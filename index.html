Our Story

Two guys.
One obsession.
Extraordinary coffee.

Hot Guys started as a bet and became a belief — that great coffee shouldn't be reserved for big cities or intimidating menus. We set up in Phagwara with a simple idea: brew honest, brew bold, and make sure everyone who walks in feels like a regular by the time they leave.

— Karan & Rohan

Our Story

Two guys.
One obsession.
Extraordinary coffee.

Great coffee shouldn’t be something you have to leave the city to find. That’s why we brought Phagwara’s first specialty coffee café to life. Our goal is simple: to introduce a coffee culture built on quality, curiosity, and community. We want to give Phagwara an experience that has long been common in bigger cities—a place where coffee is appreciated for more than just caffeine, and every visit feels worth coming back for.

— Karan & Rohan
